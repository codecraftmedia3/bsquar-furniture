About BSQUAR

Built on Skill,
Rooted in Wood

BSQUAR Carpentry & Furniture was founded on a simple belief: every piece of wood tells a story. We blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create furniture and carpentry work that transforms living and working spaces.

From custom dining tables to full kitchen cabinet installations, every project receives our full attention — from initial concept through final delivery. Our team of skilled artisans takes pride in quality that outlasts trends.
